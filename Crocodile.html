Facts about crocodiles

  • Crocodiles are reptiles
  • They have powerful jaws and sharp teeth
  • They live in rivers and swamps around the world
  • Some species can grow up to 20 feet long
  • They have an excellent sense of smell
  1. Crocodiles incubate for 3-4 months
  2. When they are born, they are somewhat independent
  3. They develop for several years
  4. They reach maturity at different ages depending on the species
  5. Some species can live for several decades
