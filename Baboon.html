Facts about Baboons

  • They are old world monkeys
  • They walk on all fours
  • They are native to the arabian peninsula
  • A group of baboons are called a troop
  • Baboons are typically unagressive towards humans
  1. Baboons go through gestation for 6 months
  2. When they are born, they are very dependent on their mother
  3. They develop for 3-4 months
  4. They fully wean after 1-2 years
  5. They fully mature at 5-8 years
